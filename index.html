ENTER DFU MODE

  1. Power on your HiChord
  2. Press and HOLD all three menu buttons simultaneously: FN1 + FN2 + FN3
  3. HiChord display will show: "Entering DFU Mode"
  4. LED will begin pulsing/blinking (Daisy bootloader mode)
  5. Connect HiChord via USB-C to computer
  6. Click "Connect" button below
  7. Browser window shows device: "Daisy Bootloader" (You may need to unplug and re-connect USB-C for this to appear. Make sure HiChord is powered on).
  8. Click "Connect" in Browser window
  9. Click "Update Firmware" button below
